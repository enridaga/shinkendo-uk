About Shinkendo UK

This site represents the England Branch of the International Shinkendo Federation, the Aikibujutsu Tanren Kenkyukai and the Kokusai Toyama Ryu Renmei organisations.

These organizations were founded and are directed by Obata Toshishiro Kaiso for the sake of promoting, supporting and overseeing Shinkendo, Aikibujutsu and Toyama Ryu schools around the world.

On this page you will find information about Shinkendo - Japanese Swordsmanship, and about its founder and headmaster, Obata Toshishiro. There is also a listing of officially licensed dojo around the England where you can study, as well as information about Aikido, Aikibujutsu and Toyama Ryu.

Shinkendo UK is dedicated to the spread of Shinkendo and Aikibujutsu in the UK.
